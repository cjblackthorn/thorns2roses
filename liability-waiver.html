Liability Waiver & Limitation of Liability

Last Updated: 2026

Please read this document carefully before participating in coaching services through Thorns2Roses Coaching.

1. Voluntary Participation

Participation in coaching services provided by Thorns2Roses Coaching is voluntary. Clients may discontinue participation at any time.

2. Coaching Services Only

Thorns2Roses Coaching provides relationship and life coaching services only.

Thorns2Roses Coaching does not provide therapy, counseling, psychiatric care, psychological treatment, medical treatment, diagnosis, legal advice, crisis intervention, or emergency services.

Participation in coaching does not establish a therapist-client, physician-patient, attorney-client, fiduciary, or emergency support relationship.

3. Client Responsibility

Clients remain fully responsible for personal decisions, actions and behaviors, communication choices, relationship decisions, emotional responses, financial decisions, legal matters, medical and mental health care, and personal safety.

Clients acknowledge that coaching discussions may involve emotionally difficult topics, relationship conflict, accountability, personal reflection, and behavioral change.

Clients agree to seek support from licensed professionals or appropriate services when their needs exceed the scope of coaching.

4. No Guarantee of Results

Thorns2Roses Coaching makes no guarantees regarding relationship outcomes, reconciliation, emotional outcomes, communication success, personal growth timelines, behavioral changes, life outcomes, financial outcomes, or interpersonal outcomes.

All coaching outcomes vary based on numerous factors outside the control of the coach.

5. Assumption of Risk

Clients understand and voluntarily accept that participation in coaching may involve emotional discomfort, stress, difficult conversations, interpersonal conflict, increased emotional awareness, changes in relationships, changes in personal boundaries, and changes in communication dynamics.

Clients voluntarily assume all risks associated with participation in coaching services.

6. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Thorns2Roses Coaching and its representatives shall not be liable for indirect damages, emotional distress, relationship outcomes, financial losses, personal decisions made by clients, actions taken or not taken by clients, third-party actions, communication disputes, technology failures, interruptions in services, or loss of electronic data.

Clients agree that participation in coaching is undertaken at their own discretion and risk.

Nothing in this waiver is intended to limit liability in cases of gross negligence, willful misconduct, or where such limitation is prohibited by law.

7. Technology & Third-Party Services

Coaching services may use third-party platforms including but not limited to Google Meet, Gmail, Google Forms, Google Sheets, Calendly, and Upheal.

Clients acknowledge that electronic communication and digital storage carry inherent privacy and security risks.

8. Crisis & Emergency Limitations

Thorns2Roses Coaching is not a crisis service, emergency support, suicide prevention, domestic violence intervention, psychiatric treatment, or medical treatment.

Clients experiencing emergencies or crisis situations should contact local emergency services or an appropriate licensed provider.

9. Release of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, clients release and hold harmless Thorns2Roses Coaching from claims, liabilities, damages, losses, or expenses arising from participation in coaching services, except in cases of gross negligence, willful misconduct, or where prohibited by law.

10. Acknowledgment

By signing or electronically acknowledging this waiver, the client confirms that they have read and understood this document, voluntarily accept participation in coaching, understand coaching is not therapy or medical care, understand no guarantees of outcomes have been made, understand participation may involve emotional discomfort, and voluntarily accept the risks associated with coaching.
