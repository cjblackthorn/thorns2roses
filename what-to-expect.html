Before Coaching Begins

What to Expect

Thorns2Roses uses a request-and-review process before coaching begins. This helps protect client safety, clarify scope, confirm fit, and keep coaching separate from therapy, counseling, medical care, psychiatric care, legal advice, or crisis intervention.

Documents You Will Need to Review and Agree To

If you are accepted as a coaching client, the intake form will ask you to acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the applicable client terms, privacy practices, liability limitations, cancellation policies, recording/transcription disclosures, electronic signature acknowledgment, and coaching consent materials listed below.

Each page includes a Save / Download as PDF option if you would like to keep a copy for your records.
