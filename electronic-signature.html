Electronic Signature & Client Acknowledgment

Last Updated: 2026

Please read this document carefully before submitting any intake, consent, or acknowledgment form related to Thorns2Roses Coaching.

1. Electronic Signature

By typing your name into a Thorns2Roses Coaching intake, consent, request, agreement, or acknowledgment form, you understand and agree that your typed name serves as your electronic signature.

Your electronic signature is intended to have the same meaning as a handwritten signature for purposes of acknowledging coaching policies, consent forms, disclosures, waivers, and related client materials.

2. Voluntary Consent

By submitting a form containing your typed name or electronic acknowledgment, you confirm that you are submitting the form voluntarily and that the information you provide is accurate to the best of your knowledge.

3. Agreement to Coaching Materials

By electronically signing or submitting intake materials, you acknowledge that you have had the opportunity to review the Thorns2Roses Coaching policies and client documents, including applicable terms and conditions, privacy practices, coaching agreement, informed consent, liability waiver, recording and transcription consent, cancellation policy, crisis and emergency policy, and related materials available on the website.

4. Incorporation by Reference

The client documents listed on the Thorns2Roses Coaching website are incorporated by reference into the intake and consent process. By acknowledging them in the intake form, you agree that these documents collectively form the expectations, boundaries, and terms under which coaching services may be provided.

5. Record of Submission

Form submissions may include a date, time, email address, typed name, and submitted responses. These records may be retained for administrative, consent, documentation, and coaching workflow purposes.

6. Right to Ask Questions

You may ask questions about any policy, disclosure, or consent document before beginning coaching services. Submitting an intake form indicates that you understand and agree to the listed materials unless otherwise discussed and documented.

7. Coaching Services Only

Electronic acknowledgment does not create a therapy, counseling, medical, psychiatric, legal, fiduciary, or emergency support relationship. Thorns2Roses Coaching provides coaching services only.

8. Acknowledgment

By typing your name and submitting an intake, consent, agreement, or acknowledgment form, you confirm that you understand your typed name acts as your electronic signature and that you agree to the applicable Thorns2Roses Coaching policies, disclosures, waivers, and consent materials.
