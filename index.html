About

Growth does not erase what hurt. It gives you somewhere to go next.

Thorns2Roses Coaching exists for people trying to build healthier relationships, communicate more honestly, understand themselves more clearly, and move through life with more intention.

Coaching is not about pretending things are easy. It is about naming patterns, building practical tools, and choosing growth without shame.

The process

Simple, grounded, and focused on real life.

01

Start with what is happening

We identify the relationship, communication, life, or self-worth patterns creating stress or keeping you stuck.

02

Clarify what needs to change

We define goals, boundaries, values, and practical shifts that help life feel more honest and workable.

03

Build tools you can use

We work on communication, reflection, and practical tools that support growth outside the session.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is coaching therapy?

No. Coaching is not therapy, psychiatric care, diagnosis, or crisis treatment. Coaching focuses on communication, goals, growth, and practical support.

Do you work with couples?

Relationship coaching may include individuals or partners depending on goals, availability, and fit.

Are sessions virtual?

Yes. This site is designed around virtual-first coaching and online scheduling.

What topics fit coaching?

Communication, boundaries, conflict patterns, self-worth, burnout, identity, life transitions, and rebuilding healthier patterns.
