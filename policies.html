Policies & Consent

Important Information Before Requesting Coaching

Coaching Services Disclaimer

Thorns2Roses provides coaching services only and does not provide therapy, counseling, psychiatric care, medical treatment, legal advice, or crisis intervention.

Coaching focuses on communication, relationships, boundaries, personal growth, self-reflection, and practical support.

Recording & Transcription Disclosure

Sessions may be recorded and transcribed using Upheal for documentation and coaching support purposes with client acknowledgment and consent.

Clients may decline recording requests when appropriate.

Crisis & Emergency Policy

Thorns2Roses Coaching is not an emergency service and is not available for crisis intervention or urgent mental health support.

If you are experiencing an emergency or require immediate assistance, contact local emergency services or a licensed mental health provider.

Client Responsibility

Clients remain fully responsible for their decisions, actions, relationships, health care, legal matters, and personal outcomes.

Coaching does not guarantee specific results or outcomes.

Cancellation Policy

Clients are expected to provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling whenever possible.

Privacy

Information submitted through intake forms, email, scheduling systems, or coaching sessions may be stored using third-party tools such as Google Workspace, Calendly, Upheal, or related business systems.
