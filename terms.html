Scope of Services

Thorns2Roses Coaching provides relationship and life coaching services focused on communication, boundaries, personal growth, relationship patterns, self-reflection, and practical support.

Thorns2Roses Coaching does not provide therapy, counseling, psychiatric care, medical treatment, diagnosis, legal advice, or crisis intervention.

Client Responsibility

Clients remain responsible for their own choices, actions, relationships, medical care, mental health care, legal matters, financial decisions, and personal outcomes.

No Guarantee of Results

Coaching may support reflection, planning, communication, and growth. Specific outcomes or results are not guaranteed.

Communication

Email and scheduling tools may be used for administrative communication. Thorns2Roses Coaching is not available for emergency or crisis response.

Scheduling and Cancellation

Clients are expected to attend scheduled sessions on time and provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellation or rescheduling whenever possible.

Technology

Services may use third-party platforms such as Google Forms, Gmail, Google Meet, Calendly, Upheal, and other business tools.

Agreement

By submitting an intake form, scheduling a session, or participating in coaching, the client acknowledges and agrees to these terms.
